<ol class="steps-list" style="margin: 4rem 0 1rem">
    <li>
       Begin in Mountain Pose, standing tall with your feet together.
    </li>
    <li>
      Shift your weight to your left foot and place your right foot on your left thigh, above your knee.
    </li>
    <li>Bring your hands together in prayer position at your heart, or extend them upwards like branches. Hold the pose for a few breaths, then release and repeat on the other side..</li>
</ol>
